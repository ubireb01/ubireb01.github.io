Sunflower

About me

Some people call me by my latin name, Helianthus and others know me as sunflower, girassol, tournesol,... Although I mostly have yellow flowers, I occasionally 'blush' in rusty red, green and white bursts of flowering patterns.

Mostly, I need sun. I'm at my best shape when my soil is fertile and when I'm in a sheltered spot, but I also thrive if I'm placed in a container. When on the ground, I grow really tall! I have throughout my live inspired many artists, with the most famous one being Van Gogh.

Agnes Dennes once planted a field of golden wheat in Manhattan and I wished I'd been there too, but it was in 1982 when I wasn't born yet and only my wheat friends made it to the plot of land.

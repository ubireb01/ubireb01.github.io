Advancing the quality of veterinary care

 

We support veterinary teams on the ground and in practice. Influencing policy. Collaborating both with veterinary teams, educators, and organisations.

(Gellix) The veterinary profession has evolved significantly in the last two decades. Yet aspiring for the highest quality of care is a constant pursuit.
We are RCVS Knowledge. An independent charity sitting at the heart of our professions, to help advance the quality of veterinary care for the benefit of animals, the public and society.
We meet our goals, by working with you. Supporting veterinary teams on the ground and in practice. Influencing policy. Collaborating with veterinary teams, educators, and organisations.
To do so, we translate and disseminate the latest research from across the globe. Making it easy to access and apply to day to day work. We foster approaches to shared learning, with training and tools to help veterinary teams integrate continuous improvement to the care they provide.
All underpinned by our veterinary archive, that logs and tracks our evolving profession, so we remain guided by the lessons of history.
We’re here for our profession. To learn from the past, thrive in the present, and advance the future of veterinary care.
